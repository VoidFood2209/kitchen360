Our Commitment to Your Health and Safety

At Kitchen 360, we take hygiene and cleanliness very seriously. Here are some of the measures we take to ensure the safety of our customers:

  • Regular sanitation of kitchen equipment and workstations
  • Frequent hand washing and glove usage by food handlers
  • Contactless delivery options to minimize person-to-person contact
  • Temperature checks for all employees
  • Enforcement of strict sick leave policies for any employees displaying symptoms
